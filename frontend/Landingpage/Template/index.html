ABOUT OUR COLLEGE

Butwal Multiple Campus is one of the biggest campus of Lumbini Zone and a first government college in Rupandehi, affiliated to Tribhuvan University (TU), Nepal.It offers different Bachelors and Masters programs with affiliation from Tribhuvan University . It was established in estd 2030, Since its establishment, the college has been running various programmes in the disciplines of Arts, Science, Commerce, Law ,ITetc . The college is located at the heart of Butwal valley,Golpark.This college has spacious grounds along with seminar hall required for each and every academic and extracurricular activities.We always welcome the new and creative ideas of students and plan and work to create practical. The aim of the College is to provide...quality education and produce competent graduates. The establishment of this Campus has helped a lot of students to pursue their bachelor and masters from a small village to a modern town in Nepal,Students are highly encouraged in their areas of interest by providing hi-tech facilities backed by faculty support. The institute places highest priority on innovative programs that include both traditional classroom theory and professional skills training.It is producing qualified students who are the backbone of the development of the nation.
